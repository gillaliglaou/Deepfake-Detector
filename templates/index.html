What are Deepfakes?

Deepfakes are synthetic media in which a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness using artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning techniques.

These videos or images can appear incredibly realistic, making it difficult to distinguish between what is real and what is fake.

While deepfake technology has many potential positive applications, it also raises concerns regarding misinformation, privacy, and security.
